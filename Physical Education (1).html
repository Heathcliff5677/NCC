Physical Education (1)

Physical Education (1) is a physical education course that integrates basic physical education theory, physical fitness and basic technical skills. The course is flexible in teaching form and diverse in teaching methods, and is suitable for developing students' comprehensive sports quality and ability. The main goals of teaching are:

1. Through the teaching of physical education theory, make students understand the meaning of health, gray health status and the health crisis of modern people, cultivate a healthy lifestyle, the positive effect of physical exercise on the human body; the principles and methods that physical exercise should follow.

2. Through the teaching of 24-style Simplified Taijiquan or Youth Changquan, cultivate students' interest in Taiji or martial arts culture, so that students can basically master the key points and offensive and defensive meanings of 24-style Simplified Taijiquan or Youth Changquan.

3. Through long-distance running practice, students can understand the technical movements and practice methods of long-distance running, and can successfully pass the 12-minute running test.

4.Through the teaching of football content, let students understand the basic skills of football dribbling, passing, stopping and kicking the ball; through the teaching of basketball content, let students understand the basic skills of basketball passing, dribbling and shooting; Teaching, so that students understand the basic skills of volleyball passing, catching and serving.
