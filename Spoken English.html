Spoken English

"Spoken English" is one of the limited elective courses for applied English majors. It is a course that integrates theory and practice based on learning situations that connect language application and language service positions. One of the basic courses.

How to study this course? This course takes the scenes in study, life, work and social activities as the main content, and relies on various social problems, including vocabulary, sentence patterns, dialogue exercises in various situations, etc., to cultivate students' fluent English expression ability, Possess a certain awareness of cross-cultural communication, so that students have the ability to live independently in foreign affairs and English communication skills in all aspects, and lay a good foundation for students to engage in language application and language services after graduation.
